• Campaign for a New Workers' Party

The Campaign for a New Workers' Party was launched in March 2006 and represents a major step forwards in the project to unite the British left within a single organisation. The Socialist Alliance has fully supported the Campaign for a New Workers' Party (CNWP) since its inception.
