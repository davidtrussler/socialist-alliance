Socialist Alliance AGM agrees to donate £100 each to anti-cuts candidates in May 2018 – if they have also been members of the SA at some time since 2004 re-launch

Applications for donations must be received by Saturday March 24th to be considered. The SA NEC will decide if criteria have been met within 7 days of receipt of applications. Please send to SA National Secretary Pete McLaren at pete.mclaren15@gmail.com

The full text of the policy agreed is as follows:

" The SA agrees to donate £100 each towards the anti-austerity local election campaigns in 2018 of any anti-austerity candidates who have been a member of the SA at any time since its re-launch in 2004/5"
